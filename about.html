Amanda Baramki is a NYC standup comedian who has been featured on AXS TV’s “Gotham Comedy Live,” "Laughs TV" on FOX, The Boston Comedy Festival, The Women In Comedy Festival, The She-Devil Comedy Festival & The NY Arab- American Comedy Festival. She's a writer for the UCB house sketch team "Nipsey," a contributing writer to Someecards and her jokes have appeared in Time Out NY as "Joke of the Week" and the NY Post's "Best Jests."
